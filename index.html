Common Questions.

Everything you need to know about working with Vetri.

How long does a typical project take?
Timelines vary by scope. Small digital assets or brand identities usually take 1-2 weeks. Complex web applications or AI integrations typically range from 4-8 weeks.
What if I'm not happy with a delivery?
Our escrow model protects you. Each milestone includes a set number of revisions. If a milestone does not meet the "Definition of Done" agreed upon in the brief, you are not required to release the funds.
Do you sign Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs)?
Yes. For high-stakes digital projects, confidentiality is often key. We are happy to sign an NDA before you share sensitive project briefs or proprietary data.
What payment methods do you accept?
As a digital-only collective, we accept Bank Transfers, Major Credit Cards via Stripe, and Crypto (USDT/USDC) for international clients. All payments are processed through our secure escrow account to ensure project safety.
How do we communicate during the project?
We use a combination of WhatsApp for quick updates, Email for formal approvals, and Video Calls (Google Meet/Zoom) for milestone presentations. You will have a dedicated point of contact for your entire project journey.
Do you offer post-project support?
Absolutely. Once a project is completed and the final milestone is released, we provide a 14-day "Bug-Free" warranty. We also offer monthly maintenance retainers for long-term technical support and updates.
Who owns the intellectual property (IP)?
You do. Upon the final release of funds for the completed project, all ownership rights and source files (Code, Design, Video) are transferred 100% to the Client.
