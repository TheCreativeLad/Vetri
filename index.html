Common Questions.

Everything you need to know about partnering with the Vetri team.

How long does a typical project take?
Timelines vary by scope. Data analysis and brand assets usually take 1-2 weeks. Complex web applications or custom AI integrations typically range from 4-8 weeks. We provide a detailed project schedule during our discovery call.
What is your Money-Back Guarantee?
For clients who choose full upfront payment, we offer a 100% Money-Back Guarantee. If the final delivery does not meet the pre-agreed "Definition of Done," you receive a full refund. Alternatively, you can opt for our Milestone Escrow where you only pay as you approve each stage.
Who is actually doing the work?
All work is managed and executed directly by the Vetri agency team. We are a specialized digital-only agency, meaning you have a dedicated Vetri Project Manager ensuring your requirements are met without any "middleman" confusion.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Bank Transfers (USD/GBP/EUR/NGN), Major Credit Cards via Stripe, and Crypto (USDT/USDC) for global clients. All financial transactions are secured through our verified agency escrow process.
How will we stay in touch?
We prioritize high-touch communication. You will have a direct line to our team via WhatsApp for daily updates, while milestone reviews and formal approvals are handled via Google Meet and Email to ensure a clear paper trail.
Who owns the final project?
You do. Upon the completion of the project and release of final payment, 100% of the Intellectual Property (IP) rights, source code, and assets are transferred to you.
