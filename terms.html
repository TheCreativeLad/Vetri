Agency Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: January 2026

The Vetri Commitment

We take full ownership of your project. You either pay per milestone after approval, or pay in full with a 100% money-back guarantee. If we don't meet the "Definition of Done," you don't pay.

1. Agency Relationship

Vetri Digital is a specialized agency. All services are performed and managed directly by the Vetri team. We do not operate as an intermediary or marketplace. By engaging us, you are entering into a direct professional services agreement with Vetri.

2. Two-Tier Payment Options

Clients may choose one of two paths for project funding:

A. Milestone Escrow

The project is divided into stages. Funds for the current stage are held in escrow and released only upon your approval of that specific milestone.

B. Full Upfront + Guarantee

You pay for the full project upfront. This track is protected by a 100% Money-Back Guarantee if the final delivery fails to meet the project brief.

3. The "Definition of Done" (DoD)

To ensure transparency, every milestone is tied to a specific Definition of Done. This is a technical checklist agreed upon before work begins. A milestone is considered complete only when all items in the DoD are verified by the Client.

4. Intellectual Property & Ownership

Upon the final release of payment (either the final milestone or the full project fee), Vetri transfers 100% ownership of all source files, code, designs, and assets to the Client. Vetri retains no rights to the work except for the right to showcase the project in our portfolio, unless an NDA specifies otherwise.

5. Refund & Guarantee Policy

  • Revision Period:

    Every delivery includes a dedicated revision phase to ensure the work matches the project brief.

  • Escrow Refund:

    If a milestone is not approved after the revision phase, the funds for that milestone remain in escrow or are returned to the client.

  • Money-Back:

    Full upfront payments are eligible for a 100% refund if the agency fails to deliver the project as defined in the scope of work.

6. Communication & Digital Approvals

As a digital-only agency, official approvals must be made via Email or WhatsApp. Approvals given through these channels are considered legally binding for the release of milestone funds.
