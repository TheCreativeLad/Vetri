You pay into a secure box. We work. You check the work. If it's good, you release the pay. If we don't deliver, you keep your money.

1. The Vetri "Trust Guarantee"

Vetri operates on a Milestone-Based Escrow Model. Our goal is to eliminate the "Trust Tax" by ensuring that clients only pay for verified, high-quality work. By engaging our services, you agree to the structured process outlined below.

2. Project Scoping & Milestones

Before any work begins, a Project Brief will be finalized.

  • All projects are divided into specific Milestones (e.g., Design Phase, Development Phase, Final Delivery).
  • Each milestone has a clear "Definition of Done" that both the Client and Vetri agree upon in writing.

3. The Escrow (Security Box) Process

01. Deposit

Clients deposit the fee for the upcoming Milestone into the Vetri Secure Account.

02. Retention

Funds are held by Vetri and are not accessible to the service team until approval.

03. Release

Once work is approved, funds for that specific milestone are released.

4. Client Review & Approval

Upon delivery of a Milestone, the Client has 3-5 business days to review the work. If the work meets the agreed-upon Scope, the Client authorizes release. If revisions are needed, they will be performed based on the original Brief at no extra cost.

5. Refund & Cancellation Policy

  • Pre-Work:

    100% refund of deposit if canceled before work starts (minus transaction fees).

  • Non-Performance:

    If we fail to meet the "Definition of Done" after revisions, escrowed funds are returned to you.
